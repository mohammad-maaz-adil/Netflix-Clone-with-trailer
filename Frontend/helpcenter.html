How to change your payment method

Important: You can change your payment method anytime. These changes take effect in your next billing cycle.

Steps to change payment method:

  1. Go to your Account page.
  2. Click Manage payment info.
  3. Select Add or Replace payment method.
  4. Enter new info, then click Save.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • PayPal
  • Netflix Gift Cards
