How to Change Your Payment Method

You can change your payment method at any time. Changes will take effect for your next billing cycle.

Steps to Change Payment Method:

  1. Go to your Account page
  2. Click Manage payment info in the Membership & Billing section
  3. Select Add payment method or Replace payment method
  4. Enter your new payment information
  5. Select Save
